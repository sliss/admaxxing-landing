The Thesis

When machines can make anything,
the only thing left that's truly scarce is you.

01

Content went to zero

Generative systems produce text, image, and video at effectively no cost. The supply of things to look at is now infinite — and infinitely cheap.

02

Attention did not

There are still only twenty-four hours, and only one of you. Human attention is the one input no model can synthesize. Its value is rising, fast.

03

So it should be yours

If your attention is the scarce asset, you should hold the title to it — and decide, on fair terms, when to spend it. Admaxxing is the ledger.
