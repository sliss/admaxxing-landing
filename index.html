⬇  Download admaxxing.dmg

macOS · 1.7 MB · unsigned early build

  1. Open the .dmg and drag admaxxing.app into your Applications folder.
  2. Clear the quarantine flag. Because this early demo isn't signed by Apple yet, macOS will block it on first launch. Open Terminal and run this once:
    $ xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/admaxxing.app
    (This just removes the “downloaded from the internet” quarantine so macOS will let the app open.)
  3. Launch Admaxxing from your Applications folder. The chyron appears in your menu bar — you're running it!
